About Our Club

Nyamira National Polytechnic Ajira Club

Established: March 2023

Members: 150+ students

The Nyamira National Polytechnic Ajira Club is part of the national Ajira Digital Program, sponsored by Ajira. We empower students with digital skills to participate in the gig economy while still in school.

Mission

To equip students with marketable digital skills for the 21st century job market

Vision

To be the leading digital skills hub in Western Kenya
